# Volumes Environment

The Volumes environment offers different analyses and processing of rock Micro-CT images. With this environment, you can perform a wide range of tasks, from applying filters to images to modeling and permeability flow simulations.

## Sections

The GeoSlicer Volumes environment is organized into several modules, each dedicated to a specific set of tasks. Click on a module to learn more about its functionalities:

*   **[Loading](/Volumes/MicroCTImport/Import.md):** Designed to import and process MicroCT images.
*   **[Filters](/Volumes/Filter/Filter.md):** Provides image filters to enhance your data and remove noise. Includes advanced filters such as Anisotropic Diffusion Gradient and Polynomial Shading Correction.
*   **[Segmentation](/Volumes/Segmentation/Segmentation.md):** Perform image segmentation using [automatic](/Volumes/Segmentation/Segmentation.md#ai-segmenter) or [manual](/Volumes/Segmentation/Segmentation.md#manual-segmentation) methods to separate different phases and features in your rock samples.
*   **[Microtom](/Volumes/Microtom/Microtom.md):** A module dedicated to simulation on microtomography data.
*   **[Microporosity](/Volumes/Microporosity/Microporosity.md):** Calculation of microporosity by generating a porosity map of your rock samples from a [segmentation](/Volumes/Microporosity/Microporosity.md#porosity-map-via-segmentation).
*   **[Modelling Flow](/Volumes/ModellingFlow/StreamlinedModelling.md):** Common workflows for microporosity calculation.
*   **[Registration](/Volumes/Register/Register.md):** Align and register multiple images into a common coordinate system.
*   **[PNM (Pore Network Modeling)](/Volumes/PNM/PNM.md):** [Extract](/Volumes/PNM/PNM.md#extractor), [simulate](/Volumes/PNM/PNM.md#simulation), and [analyze](/Volumes/PNM/PNM.md#krel-eda) pore networks from your samples. This is a powerful tool for understanding the flow properties of your samples.
*   **[Big Image](/Volumes/BigImage/BigImage.md):** Allows loading and processing large image files that cannot be loaded into memory at once.
*   **[More tools](/Volumes/MoreTools/MoreTools.md):** Explore additional tools for analysis and results visualization.
*   **[Import](/Volumes/Import/Import.md):** Imports data from standardized sources.

## What can you do?

With the GeoSlicer MicroCT environment, you can:

*   **Visualize and analyze 2D and 3D rock Micro-CT images.**
*   **Apply a wide range of image processing filters.**
*   **Segment images to identify different phases and features.**
*   **Extract and analyze pore networks.**
*   **Calculate important petrophysical properties such as porosity and permeability from simulations.**
